The 2-Minute Rule for Productivity

If a task takes less than two minutes to complete, do it immediately. This simple rule from David Allen's "Getting Things Done" helps prevent small tasks from piling up and becoming overwhelming.

Examples: replying to a short email, washing a few dishes, or making a quick phone call.
